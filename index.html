Why Choose Turkey for Your Medical Trip?

On Nova Care, we combine the best of two worlds - exceptional medical expertise and a captivating destination. We understand that choosing the right place for your medical treatment is crucial, and that's why we are committed to providing you with top-notch medical care, unparalleled guarantees, and an experience that will not only rejuvenate your health but also your soul.

Our journey takes you to the mesmerizing city of Istanbul, where you'll receive world-class medical attention while exploring the beauty and culture of this enchanting destination. Let us be your bridge to a better and healthier you, all at a fraction of the cost you'd expect. Here are the comparisons of medical procedure costs around the globe:

As you can see from the table, having a medical procedure in the US or UK can cost thousands more than in Eastern Europe. So, why choose Istanbul for your medical journey? We offer a winning combination of top-notch medical expertise, guaranteed satisfaction with the results, and significantly lower prices than other countries. Istanbul has become a hub for medical tourism due to its world-class medical facilities and skilled practitioners, making it the ideal destination for your medical treatment.

Is it really worth an extra 20 thousand to not have to get on a plane, get the best medical care, and visit one of the most antique cities, Istanbul? We understand the comfort and preference for having a procedure done in your own country, shorter travel times, and being closer to family and friends for recovery. However, these factors need to be seriously compared to the cost differences. By choosing Istanbul and our medical company, you not only save significantly but also have the chance to explore the rich history, culture, and beauty of this enchanting city during your recovery period.

When you choose us, you can be confident that you'll receive the best treatment and care. Our team is committed to providing you with personalized attention and continuous support throughout your medical journey. From helping you choose the right package for your needs to arranging accommodation and ensuring your satisfaction, our dedicated customer service will be with you every step of the way. Your health and well-being are our top priorities, and we go the extra mile to make your medical travel experience smooth, affordable, and rewarding.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of medical procedures do you assist with?

We specialize in arranging travel and accommodation for various medical procedures, including but not limited to hair transplants, cosmetic surgeries, dental treatments, and wellness retreats.

Can you help me find a suitable doctor or clinic?

Absolutely! We have partnerships with reputable doctors and clinics in Istanbul. We will connect you with experienced professionals based on your specific medical needs.

What is included in your travel and health package?

Our travel and health packages typically include round-trip flights, accommodation, airport transfers, medical appointments, and post-treatment care. Additional services can be customized based on your requirements.

How can I request a quote for a travel and health package?

To request a quote, please fill out the inquiry form on our website or contact our customer support team. Provide us with details about your desired procedure and travel preferences, and we will get back to you with a personalized package.

Can I book a travel and health package for someone else?

Yes, you can book a package on behalf of someone else. Simply provide us with the necessary details of the person who will be undergoing the medical treatment, and we will take care of the arrangements accordingly.

Are the medical facilities you work with accredited and trustworthy?

Yes, we partner with accredited and trustworthy medical facilities that meet international standards. We thoroughly assess their qualifications, experience, and patient reviews to ensure the highest quality of care for our clients.

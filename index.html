Our Services

Hair Transplantation

We offer advanced hair transplantation services to restore your natural hairline and help you regain your confidence. Our team of experienced surgeons uses state-of-the-art techniques for optimal results.

Cosmetic Surgeries

Enhance your appearance and achieve your aesthetic goals with our range of cosmetic surgery procedures. From facelifts to breast augmentations, our skilled surgeons deliver personalized and natural-looking results.

Dental Treatments

Maintain a healthy and beautiful smile with our comprehensive dental treatments. Whether you need routine cleanings, teeth whitening, or complex procedures like dental implants, our experienced dentists are here to help.

Wellness Retreats

Escape the daily stresses and rejuvenate your mind, body, and soul with our wellness retreats. Immerse yourself in serene environments, enjoy holistic therapies, and experience a transformative journey towards wellness.

Orthopedic Surgery

Our orthopedic surgery services aim to address a variety of musculoskeletal conditions and injuries. Whether you require joint replacements, spine surgeries, or minimally invasive procedures, our skilled orthopedic surgeons provide comprehensive care for optimal recovery and improved mobility.

Medical Check-ups

Our medical check-up packages offer comprehensive assessments of your overall health. With a range of diagnostic tests, consultations with specialists, and personalized health evaluations, we ensure early detection of potential health issues and provide valuable insights for maintaining your well-being.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What types of medical procedures do you assist with?

A: We specialize in arranging travel and accommodation for various medical procedures, including but not limited to hair transplants, cosmetic surgeries, dental treatments, and wellness retreats.

Q: Can you help me find a suitable doctor or clinic?

A: Absolutely! We have partnerships with reputable doctors and clinics in Istanbul. We will connect you with experienced professionals based on your specific medical needs.

Q: What is included in your travel and health package?

A: Our travel and health packages typically include round-trip flights, accommodation, airport transfers, medical appointments, and post-treatment care. Additional services can be customized based on your requirements.

Q: How can I request a quote for a travel and health package?

A: To request a quote, please fill out the inquiry form on our website or contact our customer support team. Provide us with details about your desired procedure and travel preferences, and we will get back to you with a personalized package.

Q: Can I book a travel and health package for someone else?

A: Yes, you can book a package on behalf of someone else. Simply provide us with the necessary details of the person who will be undergoing the medical treatment, and we will take care of the arrangements accordingly.

Q: Are the medical facilities you work with accredited and trustworthy?

A: Yes, we partner with accredited and trustworthy medical facilities that meet international standards. We thoroughly assess their qualifications, experience, and patient reviews to ensure the highest quality of care for our clients.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of medical procedures do you assist with?

We specialize in arranging travel and accommodation for various medical procedures, including but not limited to hair transplants, cosmetic surgeries, dental treatments, and obesity surgeries.

Can you help me find a suitable doctor or clinic?

Absolutely! We have partnerships with reputable doctors and clinics in Istanbul. We will connect you with experienced professionals based on your specific medical needs.

What is included in your travel and health package?

Our travel and health packages typically include round-trip flights, accommodation, airport transfers, medical appointments, and post-treatment care. Additional services can be customized based on your requirements. Check our packages for different options we provide.

How can I request a quote for a travel and health package?

To request a quote, please fill out the inquiry form via contact or contact with our customer support team. Provide us with details about your desired procedure and travel preferences, and we will get back to you with a personalized package.

Can I book a travel and health package for someone else?

Yes, you can book a package on behalf of someone else. Simply provide us with the necessary details of the person who will be undergoing the medical treatment, and we will take care of the arrangements accordingly.

Are the medical facilities you work with accredited and trustworthy?

Yes, we partner with accredited and trustworthy medical facilities that meet international standards. We thoroughly assess their qualifications, experience, and patient reviews to ensure the highest quality of care for our clients.

What is the recovery time for common surgeries?

Recovery times can vary based on the type of surgery. For more detailed information please check our surgeries. You will find general information and questions depending on the operation.
